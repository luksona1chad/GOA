ice cream

Ice cream is a refreshing dessert food product. It has a pleasant taste, contains proteins , fats , carbohydrates , mineral salts and vitamins. The main raw materials for making ice cream are dairy products ( milk , cream, butter , jam, chocolate , walnuts, almonds , eggs , stabilizers (agar, gelatin) and others, sugar , fruits (or berries ), aromatic substances, and others. After pasteurization and homogenization, the temperature of the ice cream is - 4-5 °C.

History

Early frozen desserts Historians believe that ice cream has existed for more than 5000 years. It was invented in a place where areas of exceptionally high temperature are bordered by areas of low temperature. 3000 years ago, in China , desserts that resemble today's ice cream decorated the table. They mixed ice and snow and added pieces of pomegranate , orange and lemon . Recipes and storage conditions year It was revealed in the book of "Shi-King" in the 16th century .

According to some sources, ice cream-like food can be found in Persia since 550 BC. [ 1 ] [ 2 ] [ 3 ] With the help of freezers, the Persians were able to make falude and sorbet all year round . [ 4 ] The letters of King Solomon of Israel describe the preservation of chilled juices. Chilled wine , juices and dairy products were used by the ancient Greeks. Hippocrates advised people to eat ice cream during illness. Alexander the Great was treated with ice cream during his campaign in India and Persia . Then they invented freezing berries in the snow. Slaves were sent to the mountains to find snow .

They immediately started adding fruit, wine, honey and milk to the water. In ancient Rome, snow was used to make soft drinks. Apicius, a famous Roman gourmet, described the making of soft drinks in his book. Nero finished his meal with cold desserts, mountain ice was added to the fruit. They used ice brought from the Alps to make it . Ice cellars were specially built. The Persian traveler, Nasri-Khozraum, wrote in 1040 that snow was brought to the table of the Sultan of Cairo from the mountainous regions of Syria to make cold drinks and ice cream. At the end of the 18th century , in ancient Russia , frozen milk was considered a national dish. Finely chopped frozen milk decorated the table.

There is a version that ice cream was first brought to Southern Europe by Marco Polo in the 14th century . He described the ice cream in his travel notes. For the first time, Italian chefs started making ice cream from European chefs. They had the shape of frozen rings. The recipe for this ice cream is close to the modern recipe. It was first made in Sicily . The first modern ice cream known to us was made with the addition of milk , sugar , chocolate , vanilla and other products in the French cafe "Tortoni". Before the invention of modern machines, ice cream was eaten only by the upper classes, at special parties. Making ice cream was very difficult at that time. Currently, ice cream is made in special freezers, the production lasts for 1-15 kg per hour. Larger quantities of ice cream can be made in ice plants.

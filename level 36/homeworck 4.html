All About Polar Bears

Explore the Life of Polar Bears

Polar bears are large carnivorous bears found in the Arctic region. Their thick white fur provides insulation and camouflage against the snow and ice. These magnificent creatures primarily hunt seals and are excellent swimmers.

Fun Facts

  • Polar bears can weigh between 900 to 1,600 pounds.
  • They can run up to 25 miles per hour on land.
  • A polar bear's skin is black, which helps absorb heat from the sun.
  • They have a layer of blubber up to 4 inches thick for insulation.

Dance Categories

Rachuli Dance

The Rachuli dance features group movements and is rooted in the traditions of the Racha region of Georgia.

Shetleruli Dance

A fast-paced and energetic style that expresses passion and vitality.

Georgian Ballet

Georgian ballet incorporates elements of traditional dances with modern influences.

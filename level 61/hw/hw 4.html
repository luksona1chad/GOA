About Spider-Man

Spider-Man is a superhero with the ability to cling to walls, enhanced strength, and agility, along with his signature web-slinging abilities.

Abilities

Spider-Man has superhuman strength, speed, and agility, along with a "spider-sense" that alerts him to danger.

Villains

Spider-Man's iconic villains include Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and Venom, who constantly challenge him.

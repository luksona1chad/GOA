About Cheetahs

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, capable of reaching speeds over 60 mph (97 km/h) in short bursts. Their agility allows them to make sharp turns while chasing prey.

Speed

Cheetahs are unrivaled when it comes to speed, capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just a few seconds.

Conservation

Sadly, cheetah populations are decreasing due to habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict. Conservation efforts are essential.

Khachapuri

Khachapuri is a traditional Georgian dish made of a soft, fluffy dough filled with melted cheese and topped with an egg. It's one of the most famous dishes in Georgia!

Khinkali

Khinkali are Georgian dumplings filled with meat, herbs, and spices. They are boiled and served with a sprinkle of black pepper. A true Georgian delicacy!

Churchkhela

Churchkhela is a traditional Georgian candy made from grape juice, nuts, and flour. It's shaped into long sticks and dried. It's often referred to as "Georgian Snickers".

Lobiani

Lobiani is a traditional Georgian bread filled with a spiced bean paste. It's a popular street food in Georgia and is often enjoyed with a glass of wine.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular street food in Georgia. It's made with grilled meat, vegetables, and a variety of sauces, all wrapped in a warm pita bread. It's a delicious and filling meal!

Salata

Salata is a traditional Georgian salad made with fresh tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and herbs. It's a light and refreshing dish that pairs well with any meal.

mwvadi

mwvadi is a traditional Georgian dish made with chicken or turkey in a rich walnut sauce. It's flavored with garlic, coriander, and other spices, and is often served with rice or bread.
